## ğŸš€ Scanning Documents

### Single Scan
1. Navigate to the **HOME** tab.
2. Click the large **Start Scan** button.
3. Your document will appear in the center preview and the right-side thumbnail panel.

### Batch Scanning
1. In the **HOME** tab, click **Batch Scan**.
2. Enter the number of pages to scan (or 0 for continuous).
3. The app will automatically trigger scans with a 2-second delay to allow you to swap pages.
